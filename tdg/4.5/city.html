city — The name of a city in an address

(#PCDATA|link|olink|ulink|emphasis|trademark|replaceable|remark|

Attributes

Common attributes

Description

The name of a city in an Address.

Processing expectations

Formatted inline. In an Address, this element may inherit the verbatim qualities of an address.

Parents

These elements contain city: address.

Children

The following elements occur in city: emphasis, indexterm, inlinegraphic, inlinemediaobject, link, olink, remark, replaceable, subscript, superscript, trademark, ulink.

address, country, email, fax, otheraddr, phone, pob, postcode, state, street.
