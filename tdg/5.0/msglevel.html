msglevel

msglevel — The level of importance or severity of a message in a message set.

Attributes

Common attributes and common linking attributes.

Description

A msglevel is part of the additional information associated with a message in a msgset. It identifies the relative importance or severity of a message.

Processing expectations

May be formatted inline or as a displayed block, depending on context. Sometimes suppressed.

On the whole, the semantics of msgset are not clearly defined.
