Attributes

Common attributes and common linking attributes.

Description

A year element identifies a year. It is used in copyright to identify the year or years in which copyright is asserted.

Processing expectations

Formatted inline.

Parents

These elements contain year: copyright.

Children

The following elements occur in year: text, abbrev, acronym, alt, anchor, biblioref, coref, date, emphasis (db._emphasis), firstterm (db._firstterm), footnote, footnoteref, foreignphrase (db._foreignphrase), glossterm (db._glossterm), indexterm (db.indexterm.endofrange), indexterm (db.indexterm.singular), indexterm (db.indexterm.startofrange), inlinemediaobject, link, olink, phrase (db._phrase), quote (db._quote), remark, subscript, superscript, trademark, wordasword, xref.
