Attributes

Additional Constraints

  • stagedir must not occur among the children or descendants of stagedir

Description

A stagedir is a stage direction in a play or other theatrical performance.

Processing expectations

Formatted as a block; sometimes as marginalia.

Attributes

None.

Parents

These elements contain stagedir: abstract, acknowledgements, answer, appendix, article, bibliodiv, bibliography, bibliolist, blockquote, callout, calloutlist, caption, caution, chapter, colophon, cover, danger, dedication, dialogue, drama, entry, example, figure, footnote, glossary, glossdef, glossdiv, glosslist, important, index, informalexample, informalfigure, itemizedlist, legalnotice, linegroup, listitem, note, orderedlist, partintro, poetry, preface, procedure, qandadiv, qandaset, question, result, revdescription, section, setindex, sidebar, simplesect, step, taskprerequisites, taskrelated, tasksummary, textobject, tip, toc, variablelist, warning.
