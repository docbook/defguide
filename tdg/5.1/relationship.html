V5.1 Assemblyrelationship

relationship — A relationship associates one or more resources.

Synopsis

relationship ::=

  • Sequence of:
    • association
    • One or more of:
      • instance

Attributes

Common attributes.

Additional attributes:

  • type (NMTOKEN)

Description

An assembly can assert that a relationship exists between two or more resources. This could be a simple “see also” relationship or a more structured path through the material. DocBook places no constraints on the type of relationships that may be expressed.

Processing expectations

Vary with the nature of the relationship.

Attributes

Common attributes.

type

Identifies the type of the relationship

Parents

These elements contain relationship: relationships.

Children

The following elements occur in relationship: association, instance.
